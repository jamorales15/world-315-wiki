Folk Magic:
- All PCs have access since they are aware of the truth of Vey. Most NPCs do not it use it unknowingly and to a lesser degree
- Folk Magic is now a default skill that is POW x 4
	- It cannot be updated with experience
	- It cannot be directly improved with experience
	- Instead spending experience on any other magic skill improves Folk Magic by the same amount

  

POW stat changes:
- Power(Vey) starts at 12? Scales by 1 every so many sessions
	- 13, 14: One Session
	- 15, 16, 17, 18: Three Sessions
	- 19+: Five Sessions + In fiction reason
- CHA no longer gives extra experience
- Using social conflict rules from Mythras Companion
- Magic Points Ave Luck points might need detached? 
- Binding is a skill to create enchanted items 
	- Stat is POW + STR
